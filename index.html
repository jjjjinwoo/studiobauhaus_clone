We are Studio Bauhaus, a web development studio based in Prague. At our core, we are developers — fusing functionalism, interaction, and aesthetics to create immersive digital experiences.

Function-First Experience

We believe that utility and function are foremost in development. Our crafting of effortless, well-polished websites starts with our keen understanding of that site's purpose.

Effortless Precision

We craft performant modules that come together quickly to form the foundation of a project. This bauhaus approach means we work efficiently at a high standard, focused on the needs of the client.

Art and Technology in Harmony

We work with an emphasis on function but with the sensibility of design. Our approach pairs technical acumen with an understanding of user experience and human-centric UX/UI design.
